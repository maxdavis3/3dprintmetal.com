About 3D PRINTMETAL

Pioneering metal additive manufacturing with precision engineering and cutting-edge technology

OUR STORY

Who We Are

3D PRINTMETAL is a specialized metal additive manufacturing service bureau based in Chicago. We operate industrial-grade DMLS and SLM systems capable of producing fully dense metal parts from a wide range of alloys including titanium, stainless steel, Inconel, and aluminum.

Our team combines deep metallurgical knowledge with advanced manufacturing expertise. From design for additive manufacturing (DfAM) consultation through final inspection, we guide every project to ensure optimal results. Whether you need a single prototype or a run of thousands, our process scales to meet your demands.

Our Mission

To make metal additive manufacturing accessible and reliable for engineers and designers across every industry. We combine state-of-the-art powder bed fusion technology with rigorous quality control to deliver parts that meet the tightest tolerances and most demanding material specifications.

Our Vision

To be the go-to partner for metal 3D printing in North America, advancing the boundaries of what’s possible in additive manufacturing. We envision a future where complex metal geometries are no longer constrained by traditional machining limitations.
