## Precision Metal Parts. Industrial-Grade Additive Manufacturing.

3D Print Metal is a specialized resource covering metal additive manufacturing for engineers, materials scientists, and procurement officers. We cover DMLS, SLM, and EBM process parameters, alloy selection, post-processing requirements, and supplier qualification with the technical depth that generalist publications miss.

Ben Stagl (SAIC professor, 20-year fabrication veteran) and Max Davis (MAXCNC) bring hands-on manufacturing expertise to the highest-stakes vertical in additive manufacturing. Metal DMLS parts at $2,000–$20,000 per build have no margin for ambiguity.

## Why Metal Additive Manufacturing

DMLS and SLM deliver properties that machining and casting cannot match:

- **Near-net-shape complexity** — internal channels, lattice infill, and topology-optimized geometry unachievable by machining alone
- **Material efficiency** — 90%+ powder recycling versus 80–95% billet waste in subtractive machining
- **Lead time compression** — from 6–12 weeks for machined titanium to 5–10 business days
- **Design freedom** — consolidate multi-part assemblies into single printed components

## Materials We Cover

- **Titanium Ti6Al4V (Grade 5)** — aerospace structures, medical implants, motorsport
- **Stainless Steel 316L & 17-4 PH** — corrosion-resistant industrial and medical components
- **Inconel 625 & 718** — high-temperature turbine and energy applications
- **Aluminum AlSi10Mg** — lightweight aerospace and automotive parts
- **Cobalt Chrome CoCrMo** — dental prosthetics, orthopedic implants
- **Tool Steel H13 & Maraging** — injection mould tooling, conformal cooling inserts

## Process Ecosystem

- **EOS M290, M400** — industry standard for Ti6Al4V DMLS in aerospace supply chains
- **Trumpf TruPrint 1000, 3000** — compact and mid-size platforms for medical device production
- **SLM Solutions 280, 500** — multi-laser productivity for high-volume aerospace components
- **Post-processing:** HIP, vacuum heat treatment, CNC hybrid machining, electropolishing
